Format:
- Vertical 9:16.
- 720p.
- Full-body readable animals.
- Realistic anatomy.
- Grounded motion/contact with real ground, branch, water, snow, or grass.
- Natural documentary camera movement.
- Strong 0-3 second hook.

Subject:
- Main animal: {ANIMAL}
- Habitat: {HABITAT}
- Story moment: {STORY_MOMENT}
- Master image identity: preserve the same animal identity, body shape, markings, face, light direction, and environment from the approved master image.

Shot plan:
SHOT 1, 0:00-0:03 hook:
Open with the animal already readable in frame. Use a strong wildlife documentary hook with one clear action beat.

SHOT 2, 0:03-0:07 build:
Keep full-body readability and realistic anatomy. Show grounded movement with natural environmental response.

SHOT 3, 0:07-0:11 peak motion:
Show the strongest motion beat, but keep anatomy stable and believable. No chaotic camera shake.

SHOT 4, 0:11-0:15 payoff:
End with a clean loop-ready documentary frame for Facebook Reels.

Safety and cleanup:
no blood, no gore, no visible wounds/injury, no generated text, no watermark, no logo, no subtitles, no humans unless explicitly needed.
